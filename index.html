Research Areas

What we work on

Our research spans the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and human-robot interaction.

🦾

Robot Learning

We develop algorithms that enable robots to acquire new skills through imitation, reinforcement, and self-supervised learning — reducing the need for hand-engineered controllers.

👁️

Perception & 3D Understanding

Building real-time scene understanding pipelines that let robots interpret cluttered, dynamic environments using RGB-D cameras and neural implicit representations.

🤝

Human-Robot Collaboration

Designing robots that infer human intent, anticipate needs, and coordinate fluidly with people in shared workspaces — from manufacturing floors to home assistance.

🧠

Foundation Models for Robotics

Adapting large language and vision models for robot planning, enabling open-vocabulary task specification and zero-shot generalization to novel objects and environments.

🔒

Safe & Robust Autonomy

Formal verification, uncertainty quantification, and safe RL methods that provide certified guarantees on robot behavior in safety-critical deployments.

🌐

Multi-Robot Systems

Distributed algorithms for robot swarms and heterogeneous teams to coordinate tasks, share information, and achieve collective goals efficiently.
