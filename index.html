Research Areas

What we work on

Our research spans the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and human-robot interaction.

🚁

Hybrid Aerial-Terrestrial Robot

Robots that combine walking and flying to inspect 3D structures like bridges without being time-consuming, using LQG control for vibration compensation during flight.

👁️

Robot Vision & SLAM

Thermal image-based SLAM and illumination-invariant camera localization using Real-to-Synthetic Transform (REST), achieving ~30% improvement in feature matching accuracy.

🤝

Human-Robot Interaction

Social navigation models that interpret human intentions during robot encounters, and gesture-based drone control for intuitive human-drone collaboration (ICRA 2021).

🔥

Sensor Fusion & Vision Augmentation

VisMerge combines thermal and RGB video for low-light environments, reducing latency registration error by 87% through AR time-warping algorithms.

🥊

Haptic Interface

Encountered-type haptic display using drones as flying motion bases, enabling mid-air touch feedback with lightweight paper end-effectors shaped by airflow.

☁️

Cloud Robotics

Robot-to-robot collaboration through cloud services, enabling distributed intelligence and monitoring of human activities across networked robotic systems.
